# 🀄 Mahjong House Rules: The Exponential Edition

## 1. Winning Structure & Special Hands
The **Minimum Fan (Mok)** required to win is determined by the group consensus before each game session starts.

### Standard Hand
* **14 Tiles:** Consists of **4 Sets + 1 Pair**.
* **Sets:** Can be a "Chow" (Run/Sequence) or "Pung" (Triplet).

### Special Hands (Allowed)
* **Seven Pairs:** A hand consisting of 7 different pairs. 
* **Thirteen Orphans:** One of each "Terminal" (1 & 9 of each suit) and one of each "Honor" (all Winds and Dragons), plus one extra tile to form a pair.

---

## 2. Hand Scoring (Fan Counting)
* **Common Hand (Ping Hu):** 1 Fan
* **All Pungs (All Triplets):** 3 Fan
* **Mixed One Suit:** One suit + Winds/Dragons = 3 Fan
* **Full One Suit:** All 14 tiles are from the same suit = 7 Fan
* **Dragon Pung:** +1 Fan per set
* **Small Three Dragons:** 5 Fan
* **Great Three Dragons:** 8 Fan (Limit)

---

## 3. Flowers, Seasons & "Biting" (Kud)
* **Basic Flower Score:** Every Flower or Season tile you possess adds **+1 Fan each** (e.g., 4 tiles = +4 Fan).
* **The "Biting" (Kud) Rule:** * If you win and have a **Season** tile with a specific number (e.g., #1), and an opponent has the **Flower** tile with the same number (#1), that opponent is "Bitten."
    * **Penalty:** The "Bitten" opponent must pay an **additional 1 Fan** specifically to you (effectively doubling their personal payout).
* **No Flower Bonus:** None. There are no points awarded for winning with zero flowers.

---

## 4. Payout & The Exponential Rule
**Formula:** $Price = 1 \times 2^{n}$ (where $n$ is the total Fan count).

* **Self-Draw (Zi Mo):** * Automatically adds **+1 Fan** to your total score (doubling the payout).
    * **Everyone** at the table pays you the calculated price.
* **Win by Discard (The Shooter):** * The player who discarded the winning tile (The Shooter) pays **Double** the calculated price.
    * The other two players pay the standard calculated price.

---

## 5. The "Bao" Penalty (Full Liability)
If you discard a tile that allows a player to complete a high-value hand that was already "visible" on the table, you are **liable for the entire table's losses**. You pay the sum of what all three players would have owed.

**Bao applies when a player already has the following exposed:**
1.  **Full One Suit:** Player has 3 sets of the same suit exposed.
2.  **Great Three Dragons:** Player has 2 Dragon sets exposed.
3.  **Great Four Winds:** Player has 3 Wind sets exposed.
4.  **All Pungs:** Player has 3 "Pung" (Triplet) sets exposed.

---

## 6. Game Rotation
* **1 Full Game:** Consists of 4 Rounds (East, South, West, North).
* **The Dealer (East Seat):** If the dealer wins or the game is a draw, the dealer stays ("Lian"). If the dealer loses, the position rotates to the left.
